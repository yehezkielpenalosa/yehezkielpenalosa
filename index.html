THE TRICHOTOMY POSTERS

The Trichotomy Poster is a series of posters divided into three main parts, namely The Body, The Soul, and The Spirit. Each of these posters is a form of visualization of the 5 chapters of the bonsai novel on the theme of discrimination.

Scroll for More

Hello, welcome to the world of visualization created by typography enthusiast, Yehezkiel Penalosa. This world will present an exploration of typography in VR, that comes from the visualization of a novel.

Novel Bonsai by Pralampita Lembahmata, tells the acts of discrimination experienced by the Chinese Benteng, including: the tragedy of the Dutch invasion, Japanese occupation, and even the gloomy story of May 1998. More specifically, this novel also tells of the suffering or prejudice experienced by the Chinese Benteng descendants of four generations, namely Boenarman, Boenadi, Meily, and Feily.
